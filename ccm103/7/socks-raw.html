Silver Socks

The odorless socks that's made with pure silver

We're making socks using pure silver and merino wool - insanely comfortable, flawless performance, and naturally odorless.

Try a Pair

Next Level Comfort

We took comfort to a whole new level by creating our own fabric with a blend of Merino Wool that feels ultra soft, wicks moisture, and regulates temperature. Try and you'll never go back to anything else.

Permanent Freshness

We blend pure silver into our socks to prevent stink from accumulating. The silver never washes away and will perform for the life of the product, keeping your socks fresh and clean at all times.

Simply Convenient

Going on a two week journey? A pair of socks are all you need. From the work place to the gym to the outdoors, we designed our socks to perform no matter where you go.

Look Sharp

We designed our socks to be minimal and sexy, despite being packed with performance. Go on, roll those cuffs and show em' off because you got the best looking feet in the block.

How Silver Socks Fights Odor

Our fabrics are woven with pure silver, which creates an ionic shield against odor. It's effective and designed to last the life of the product.

Sweat itself is mostly water, and does not have any smell.

But the sweat on certain parts of your body is high in proteins that smell.

The silver in our gear impedes the smelly proteins to keep them fresh.
